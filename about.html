Dogcare is a technology company that serves in the field of animal health consultation. Through Dogcare, people can consult with a veterinarian via smartphone anytime, anywhere for 24 hours. Dogcare's goal is to facilitate access to consultations for dogs that need health care.


Dogcare users can search for a doctor online to talk about their dog's health condition. Then, after the doctor diagnoses the symptoms and the doctor can provide appropriate medication.
